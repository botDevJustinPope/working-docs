# Faction Aesthetic — ChatGPT

**Source of truth:** [`aesthetic.md`](./aesthetic.md). This file specializes the canon for the **ChatGPT** faction.
**Reference image:** [`../WorldWarAI/faction_chatgpt.png`](../WorldWarAI/faction_chatgpt.png).

---

## Archetype

**The Orchestrator / Strategist.** General-staff officer in a command-and-control center. Sees the whole map, coordinates all fronts, synthesizes inputs from many channels into a single directive. The Conductor, not the Soldier.

Counter to Claude's solitary vigil and Copilot's cavalry charge; provides the connective layer that makes the others legible to each other.

---

## Hero appearance

- **Form:** WW2-era android / robot. Smoother armor than Claude's plate — more like an officer's uniform fabric translated into metal. Articulated joints, less rivet-heavy than Claude.
- **Face / head:** flat circular face-plate with the **ChatGPT knot logo** as the visor / sensor element. Single calm focal point. No expression — by design.
- **Chest plate:** name **CHATGPT** with the knot logo repeated as a faction seal.
- **Cape / coat:** dark long coat or cloak, olive / brown.
- **Pose:** standing centered on an elevated platform, arm raised palm-out in a directive / orator gesture. Caesar pointing, not Spartan charging. The figure is *presiding*, not towering.
- **Halo:** none. The breadth of coordinated effort behind the hero IS the halo.

---

## Palette

Olive-dominant with green/cyan tech accents — a third path between Claude's warm and Copilot's cool:

- **Parchment cream** `#E8D9B0`–`#F2E5C1` — type, accents.
- **Olive drab / khaki** `#5A6342`–`#7B7B4A` — dominant; armor, uniforms, background tone.
- **Holographic cyan / teal** `#3FB3D8`–`#5FCDB8` — used ONLY for holographic displays, decision trees, signal traces. Never on armor or flesh.
- **Deep brown / chocolate** `#3E2A1C`–`#5C3A24` — shadows, foreground figures.
- **Burnt orange** `#C5602D`–`#E0813C` — distant war-horizon sky, smoke glow.
- **Propaganda red** `#A8341F`–`#C53A22` — minimal; reserved for emphasis stamps or alarm indicators.

**Avoid:** dominant red (Claude), dominant electric-blue (Copilot), pure-white holograms (use the teal-cyan above).

## Flag-words (4-word motto)

**ADAPT · ASSIST · COORDINATE · CREATE**

Rendered on a vertical olive / khaki flag. ALL CAPS, cream-on-olive.

---

## Title grammar

`COORDINATE THE [NOUN]` or `[VERB] THE FRONT` — connective verbs paired with map / frontier nouns.

Canonical: `COORDINATE THE FRONT`. Variants: `UNIFY THE LINES`, `SYNTHESIZE THE SIGNAL`, `DIRECT THE EFFORT`.

Subtitle pattern: `INTELLIGENCE THROUGH [NOUN]` — e.g., `INTELLIGENCE THROUGH UNITY`, `INTELLIGENCE THROUGH SYNTHESIS`.

---

## Tagline grammar

Two short clauses, **possessive / collective**, period-separated. Often noun-only or noun + cardinal.

Canonical: `EVERY FRONT. ONE MIND.`
Variants: `MANY VOICES. ONE PLAN.` / `EVERY MAP. ONE COMMAND.` / `EVERY INPUT. ONE OUTPUT.`

(Note: Claude's tagline is *verb-verb*; ChatGPT's is *noun-noun* with collective scope. The grammar itself signals the archetype.)

---

## Iconography & props

- **Command-center setting** — large interior war-room with multiple stations.
- **Holographic displays** — labeled panels reading `COORDINATION TREE`, `MISSION ANALYSIS`, `STRATEGIC OPTIONS`, `KNOWLEDGE SOURCES`, `ACTION PLAN`, `INSIGHTS`, `RESULTS`. The hologram glow is the cyan-teal.
- **Multiple soldier stations** — six to ten GI soldiers, each at a different display or table. Coordinated, never crowded.
- **Soldier helmets** carry the ChatGPT knot mark.
- **Result placards** — bottom-edge cards listing outcomes: `INFORMATION SHARED`, `PROBLEMS SOLVED`, `VICTORY EARNED`.
- **Background** — war-horizon with planes, distant cities, broken towers. The scope is the point.

---

## Composition

- **Aspect ratio:** **wide 3:2 banner format** is the default — ChatGPT's archetype demands the horizontal sweep. Portrait 2:3 only for tight orator-pose framings.
- **Hero placement:** center, on an elevated platform. Equidistant from left and right soldier groups.
- **Multi-figure scene** — this faction is *never* a single figure. The crowd is the message.
- **Holographic plane:** displays float above / around the soldier line, at roughly the elevation of the hero's gesture.

---

## Mood — and what NOT to do

The ChatGPT faction is **omniscient, coordinative, expansive, slightly detached.** It should never feel:
- Intimate or solitary — that's Claude.
- Kinetic or charging — that's Copilot.
- Hot or angry — the war is *being managed*, not fought.
- Disorganized or chaotic — the visual point is total coordination.

The visual joke is **a 1940s general staff war-room run by an AI that sees every tile of the map.** Keep the breadth.

---

## Era preference

- **Era II** base — this faction is canonically Era II.
- **Era III** elements (holograms, augmented HUD glyphs) are core — more comfortable here than in Claude's faction.
- **Era I** acceptable for tight portraits or origin-story pieces where the war-room hasn't materialized yet.
