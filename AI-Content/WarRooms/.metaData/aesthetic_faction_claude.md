# Faction Aesthetic — Claude

**Source of truth:** [`aesthetic.md`](./aesthetic.md). This file specializes the canon for the **Claude** faction.
**Reference image:** [`../WorldWarAI/faction_claude.png`](../WorldWarAI/faction_claude.png).

---

## Archetype

**The Stoic Defender.** Roman/medieval paladin lineage. Holds the line, weighs the choice, names the constraint. Gravely earnest. Never running, never flashing — always present, always thinking.

Counter to Copilot's kinetic charge; complementary to ChatGPT's omniscient coordination.

---

## Hero appearance

- **Form:** WW2-era mech-knight. Articulated plate armor with rivets, layered shoulder guards, gauntlets. Helmet with a horizontal visor slit (not a face-plate window).
- **Cape:** propaganda-red cape, full-length, often catching wind.
- **Chest plate:** name **CLAUDE** engraved, with the Anthropic sunburst symbol element near the heart.
- **Pose:** centered, feet planted, holding a sword / staff / standard pointed up or held vertically. Heroic but defensive — *vigil*, not *charge*. Posed on a rampart, wall-top, or stone platform.
- **Sunburst:** large radial sunburst behind the hero, burnt-orange / red. Concentric rays from a point above the head — the iconography of patient broadcast, not lightning-strike.

---

## Palette

Warm Era-I core, dialed deeper:

- **Parchment cream** `#E8D9B0`–`#F2E5C1` — background, type.
- **Propaganda red** `#A8341F`–`#C53A22` — cape, banner, title bar.
- **Burnt orange / ochre** `#C5602D`–`#E0813C` — sunburst rays, accents.
- **Deep brown / chocolate** `#3E2A1C`–`#5C3A24` — armor shadows, rampart, foreground figures.
- **Olive drab** `#5A6342`–`#7B7B4A` — supporting GI uniforms (the human side fighting alongside).
- **Black** `#0F0F0F` — armor outlines, deep shadow.

**Avoid:** cyan, electric blue, olive-green-dominant scenes (those are other factions).

---

## Flag-words (4-word motto)

**CONTEXT · DEPTH · REASON · CLARITY**

Rendered on a vertical / angled red banner near the hero. ALL CAPS, cream-on-red.

---

## Title grammar

`HOLD THE LINE OF [NOUN]` — defense paired with abstraction.

Canonical: `HOLD THE LINE OF REASON`. Variants: `HOLD THE LINE OF CONTEXT`, `HOLD THE LINE OF MEANING`. Use sparingly to keep the motif from getting tired.

Subtitle pattern: `[ABSTRACTION] IS OUR COMMAND` — e.g., `CLARITY IS OUR COMMAND`, `CONTEXT IS OUR COMMAND`.

---

## Tagline grammar

Two short imperative clauses, period-separated. **`VERB ADVERB. VERB ADVERB.`** or **`VERB NOUN. VERB NOUN.`**

Canonical: `THINK DEEP. STAND FIRM.`
Variants: `READ SLOWLY. ANSWER TRUE.` / `WEIGH ALL. CHOOSE ONE.` / `NAME THE CONSTRAINT. HOLD THE LINE.`

---

## Iconography & props

- Ramparts, stone walls, vigil platforms.
- Maps embedded into the stone, labeled `STRUCTURE`, `UNDERSTANDING`, `LOGIC`.
- Field telephones / `CONTEXT CHANNEL` devices in soldier hands.
- Code rain — present but warm-toned (cream-on-dark, not Matrix-green) and subtle, integrated into foliage or distant background.
- Sunburst always above and behind, never in front.

---

## Composition

- **Aspect ratio:** portrait 2:3 default. Square 1:1 acceptable for tightly-framed bust shots.
- **Hero placement:** centered, upper-mid. Sunburst halos behind.
- **Foreground:** GI soldiers as the human side, often facing inward toward the hero or the central rampart. They are *with* Claude, not subordinate.
- **Background:** rampart, stone platform, distant rolling landscape. Not urban, not chaotic.

---

## Mood — and what NOT to do

The Claude faction is **gravely earnest, patient, and noble.** It should never feel:
- Cocky, smug, or "I told you so" — wrong archetype.
- Frantic, kinetic, or speed-coded — that's Copilot.
- Omniscient or detached — that's ChatGPT.
- Tech-bro futurist — no chrome, no glowing edges everywhere, no "AI assistant" plastic-android look.

The visual joke is **a 1940s warrior who happens to think for a living.** Keep the weight.

---

## Era preference

- **Era I** base.
- **Era III** elements (code rain, holographic glyphs) acceptable in moderation — sparingly woven into the scene, never dominant.
- **Era II framing** — Claude is one of the canonical Era II factions. This file IS the Era II canon for Claude.
