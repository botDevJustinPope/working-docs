# Faction Aesthetic — GitHub Copilot

**Source of truth:** [`aesthetic.md`](./aesthetic.md). This file specializes the canon for the **GitHub Copilot** faction.
**Reference image:** [`../WorldWarAI/faction_githubcopilot.png`](../WorldWarAI/faction_githubcopilot.png).

---

## Archetype

**The Cavalry Charge.** Fast, kinetic, accelerationist. Closes the distance. Suggests, accepts, ships. Defeats *manual toil, repetitive tasks, blank screens*. The Sprinter, not the Strategist.

Counter to Claude's deliberate vigil and ChatGPT's panoramic coordination; provides the closing-speed that the other two factions deliberately renounce.

---

## Hero appearance

- **Form:** WW2-era cyber-robot — blockier than Claude's plate armor, more aggressive than ChatGPT's smooth uniform-bot. Forward-leaning torso, low center of gravity, large boots for impact.
- **Head:** glowing **cyan goggle-visor** (single wide horizontal slit, electric-cyan glow). No faceplate features — the goggles are the face.
- **Chest plate:** name **COPILOT** with the **GitHub Octocat mark**.
- **Pose:** mid-charge, low to the ground, motion lines trailing. Never standing still. Often a second cyber-robot ally charging in the mid-ground — Copilot is a *unit*, not a hero.
- **Wings / cape:** none. The motion lines and code-rain trails *are* the cape.

---

## Palette

Cool steel core — the deliberate counterpoint to Claude's warm palette:

- **Steel navy** `#1E2A3A` — dominant; armor, foreground figures, sky.
- **Electric blue** `#1F5FA8` — armor highlights, motion lines, code rain.
- **Cyan glow** `#3FB3D8` — goggles, accept-prompts, suggestion overlays; the brightest accent.
- **Parchment cream** `#E8D9B0`–`#F2E5C1` — type, title bars (the aged-paper feel survives even in this palette).
- **Burnt orange** `#C5602D`–`#E0813C` — sparingly; distant smoke, faint horizon glow. Used to keep the propaganda-poster heritage visible.
- **Black** `#0F0F0F` — outlines, deep shadow.

**Avoid:** propaganda red (Claude), olive-green-dominant scenes (ChatGPT), pure-green Matrix-style code rain (warm-bias the cyan rain so it reads as aged propaganda, not Hollywood cyberpunk).

## Flag-words (4-word motto)

**SPEED · SUGGESTION · FLOW · EXECUTION**

Rendered on an angled / wind-caught blue banner near the hero. ALL CAPS, cream-on-navy.

---

## Title grammar

`ADVANCE AT [NOUN]` or `[VERB] AT SPEED` — motion verbs paired with rate / momentum nouns.

Canonical: `ADVANCE AT SPEED`. Variants: `STRIKE AT SPEED`, `SHIP AT SPEED`, `ADVANCE AT FLOW`.

Subtitle pattern: `[NOUN] WINS THE DAY` — e.g., `MOMENTUM WINS THE DAY`, `SPEED WINS THE DAY`.

---

## Tagline grammar

Two short imperative clauses, **comparative or accelerating**, period-separated. **`VERB ADVERB. VERB COMPARATIVE-ADVERB.`**

Canonical: `MOVE FAST. BUILD FASTER.`
Variants: `WRITE FAST. SHIP FASTER.` / `THINK QUICK. ACT QUICKER.` / `STRIKE NOW. STRIKE AGAIN.`

(Note: Claude's tagline is *verb-verb* with depth. Copilot's is *verb-verb* with acceleration. Same shape, opposite vector.)

---

## Iconography & props

- **Code rain** — blue / cyan, falling like sparks. Snippets of real code visible: `for (...)`, `if (...)`, `const result`, `def calculate`, `@autocomplete`. The code IS the weather.
- **Suggestion boxes** — small floating panels: `✓ accept`, `// suggestion`, autocomplete carets.
- **Helmet stamps** — soldier helmets marked `COMMIT`, `DEPLOY`, `MERGE`, `PUSH`. The grunt-side of the faction is shipping, not thinking.
- **The ENTER key** — large physical `ENTER` key as an iconographic motif, frequently in the lower foreground.
- **Defeated nouns** — words like `MANUAL TOIL`, `REPETITIVE TASKS`, `BLANK SCREENS` floating in negative space, struck through or being trampled. Copilot defines itself by what it kills.
- **Soldiers at laptops** — not maps, not radios. Laptops glowing blue.

---

## Composition

- **Aspect ratio:** portrait 2:3 default. The vertical frame supports the *charging-down-the-frame* motion.
- **Hero placement:** mid-frame, leaning forward / downward. Motion vectors point toward the viewer or the lower-right.
- **Diagonals** — Copilot compositions love diagonal lines. Avoid the static centered symmetry that defines Claude.
- **Action scene** — never a vigil, never a war-room. A *moment of charge*.

---

## Mood — and what NOT to do

The Copilot faction is **kinetic, accelerationist, slightly relentless.** It should never feel:
- Contemplative or paused — that's Claude.
- Coordinative or overseeing — that's ChatGPT.
- Tired, defeated, or grinding — Copilot's failure mode is "burnout"; the visual must always feel like fresh momentum.
- Generic-cyberpunk — keep the WW2-propaganda heritage in the type, the texture, the framing. The aged-paper feel survives even in this cooler palette.

The visual joke is **a 1940s cavalry charge fought with autocomplete.** Keep the speed.

---

## Era preference

- **Era II** base — this faction is canonically Era II.
- **Era III** elements (code rain, cyber-armor, HUD glyphs) are core — Copilot lives further along the era arc than Claude.
- **Era I** unsuitable for solo Copilot scenes — the static medium fights the archetype.
