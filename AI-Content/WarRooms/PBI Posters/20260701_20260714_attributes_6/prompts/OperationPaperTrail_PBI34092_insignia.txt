**Operation insignia — circular mission patch (Teams thread image).**

**Format:** A single circular military unit insignia / mission patch, centered and filling the frame on a flat dark-charcoal or transparent background, sized to crop cleanly into a round Teams avatar. Square 1:1 canvas. Bold and iconographic — it must stay legible at small avatar size.

**Style:** WW2-era squadron embroidered-patch / stamped-leather emblem fused with subtle retro-futurist cyberpunk. Aged, lightly distressed, with a stitched double-border rim. Palette: parchment cream (#E8D9B0–#F2E5C1), burnt orange (#C5602D–#E0813C), olive drab (#5A6342–#7B7B4A), propaganda red (#A8341F–#C53A22), deep brown (#3E2A1C). Sparing electric-cyan glow (#3FB3D8) as an Era III accent only — a thin highlight, never dominant. No photorealism, no modern flat-design pastels, no pure-white background.

**Rim:** an outer ring carrying curved stencil ALL-CAPS text — **'OPERATION PAPER TRAIL'** across the top arc and **'ON THE RECORD'** across the bottom arc — cream lettering on a propaganda-red ring, with a small five-point star separating the arcs on each side. A small olive tab at the very bottom reads **'PBI 34092'**.

**Center emblem:** a **typed report page feeding out of a field typewriter / teletype platen** as the hero device — the page shows an option followed by a couple of typed **'ATTRIBUTE : VALUE'** lines. Beside the platen, a small **gating lever stencilled 'ON'** (the feature flag that lets the attribute lines print) with a faint cyan ready-glow. A short **paper trail / carbon-copy edge** curls off the bottom of the page. Essence: the choices captured in writing on the report — but only when the flag is on.

**Unit DNA (shared across the set):** integrate a small parachute-over-drop-zone-marker mark — the VDS Pathfinder Detachment emblem — as a subtle motif near the bottom-center of the disc, with a faint cyan glow on the parachute shroud lines. Keep it small; the typed-report central emblem dominates.

**Critical:** one or two symbols maximum (the typed report page + the flag lever), no human figures, no scene clutter. It must read instantly as *attribute values typed onto an official report*, not a generic document or printer icon. Keep the cyan confined to the flag-lever glow — do not let it spread into the field; the warm parchment / burnt-orange / olive / propaganda-red backbone must dominate. The aged-paper / embroidered-patch finish must survive — if it reads as a glossy modern app icon or Cyberpunk fan art, pull the neon back and push the propaganda-patch framing forward.
