**Operation insignia — circular mission patch (Teams thread image).**

**Format:** A single circular military unit insignia / mission patch, centered and filling the frame on a flat dark-charcoal or transparent background, sized to crop cleanly into a round Teams avatar. Square 1:1 canvas. Bold and iconographic — it must stay legible at small avatar size.

**Style:** WW2-era squadron embroidered-patch / stamped-leather emblem fused with subtle retro-futurist cyberpunk. Aged, lightly distressed, with a stitched double-border rim. Palette: parchment cream (#E8D9B0–#F2E5C1), burnt orange (#C5602D–#E0813C), olive drab (#5A6342–#7B7B4A), propaganda red (#A8341F–#C53A22), deep brown (#3E2A1C). Sparing electric-cyan glow (#3FB3D8) as an Era III accent only — a thin highlight, never dominant. No photorealism, no modern flat-design pastels, no pure-white background.

**Rim:** an outer ring carrying curved stencil ALL-CAPS text — **'OPERATION FIELD REPORT II'** across the top arc and **'SHOW THE PICTURE'** across the bottom arc — cream lettering on a propaganda-red ring, with a small five-point star separating the arcs on each side. A small olive tab at the very bottom reads **'PBI 34091'**.

**Center emblem:** a **report page bearing photographs** as the hero device — a stamped report sheet showing one **large framed 'MAIN' photo-plate** at the top and a **row of two or three smaller thumbnail photo-plates** beneath it, a faint cyan ready-glow along the main frame. A small **roman-numeral 'II'** is stamped in a corner of the sheet (the sequel / iteration 2). Essence: the report graduates from text tags to actual pictures — first image is the main, the rest are thumbnails.

**Unit DNA (shared across the set):** integrate a small parachute-over-drop-zone-marker mark — the VDS Pathfinder Detachment emblem — as a subtle motif near the bottom-center of the disc, with a faint cyan glow on the parachute shroud lines. Keep it small; the report-with-photos central emblem dominates.

**Critical:** one or two symbols maximum (the report sheet with its main image + thumbnail strip), no human figures, no scene clutter. It must read instantly as *a report carrying photographs (a main image plus thumbnails)*, not a generic photo album or gallery app. Keep the cyan confined to the main-frame highlight — do not let it spread into the field; the warm parchment / burnt-orange / olive / propaganda-red backbone must dominate. The aged-paper / embroidered-patch finish must survive — if it reads as a glossy modern app icon or Cyberpunk fan art, pull the neon back and push the propaganda-patch framing forward.
