**Operation insignia — circular mission patch (Teams thread image).**

**Format:** A single circular military unit insignia / mission patch, centered and filling the frame on a flat dark-charcoal or transparent background, sized to crop cleanly into a round Teams avatar. Square 1:1 canvas. Bold and iconographic — it must stay legible at small avatar size.

**Style:** WW2-era squadron embroidered-patch / stamped-leather emblem fused with subtle retro-futurist cyberpunk. Aged, lightly distressed, with a stitched double-border rim. Palette: parchment cream (#E8D9B0–#F2E5C1), burnt orange (#C5602D–#E0813C), olive drab (#5A6342–#7B7B4A), propaganda red (#A8341F–#C53A22), deep brown (#3E2A1C). Sparing electric-cyan glow (#3FB3D8) as an Era III accent only — a thin highlight, never dominant. No photorealism, no modern flat-design pastels, no pure-white background.

**Rim:** an outer ring carrying curved stencil ALL-CAPS text — **'OPERATION TOLL GATE'** across the top arc and **'CHECK THE PASS'** across the bottom arc — cream lettering on a propaganda-red ring, with a small five-point star separating the arcs on each side. A small olive tab at the very bottom reads **'PBI 30833'**.

**Center emblem:** a **checkpoint boom-barrier / toll gate** as the hero device — a striped barrier arm across a short stretch of road, stencilled **'CONTRACT'** on the arm. Held up beside or in front of it, an **'ELEVATION' pass** rendered as a small stamped **house-front elevation blueprint card** with a faint cyan ready-glow. A tiny **pennant flag** on the gatepost carries an **'ON'** toggle (the require-elevation rule armed). Essence: no pass, no passage — the gate lifts only when the required elevation is presented.

**Unit DNA (shared across the set):** integrate a small parachute-over-drop-zone-marker mark — the VDS Pathfinder Detachment emblem — as a subtle motif near the bottom-center of the disc, with a faint cyan glow on the parachute shroud lines. Keep it small; the gate-and-pass central emblem dominates.

**Critical:** one or two symbols maximum (the barrier + the elevation pass), no human figures, no scene clutter. The barrier-plus-pass must read instantly as *a checkpoint that demands the required credential*, not a generic railroad crossing. Keep the cyan confined to the pass glow and the flag toggle — do not let it spread into the field; the warm parchment / burnt-orange / olive / propaganda-red backbone must dominate. The aged-paper / embroidered-patch finish must survive — if it reads as a glossy modern app icon or Cyberpunk fan art, pull the neon back and push the propaganda-patch framing forward.
