**Operation insignia — circular mission patch (Teams thread image).**

**Format:** A single circular military unit insignia / mission patch, centered and filling the frame on a flat dark-charcoal or transparent background, sized to crop cleanly into a round Teams avatar. Square 1:1 canvas. Bold and iconographic — it must stay legible at small avatar size.

**Style:** WW2-era squadron embroidered-patch / stamped-leather emblem fused with subtle retro-futurist cyberpunk. Aged, lightly distressed, with a stitched double-border rim. Palette: parchment cream (#E8D9B0–#F2E5C1), burnt orange (#C5602D–#E0813C), olive drab (#5A6342–#7B7B4A), propaganda red (#A8341F–#C53A22), deep brown (#3E2A1C). Sparing electric-cyan glow (#3FB3D8) as an Era III accent only — a thin highlight, never dominant. No photorealism, no modern flat-design pastels, no pure-white background.

**Rim:** an outer ring carrying curved stencil ALL-CAPS text — **'OPERATION WHEELS UP'** across the top arc and **'CLEAR THE RAMP'** across the bottom arc — cream lettering on a propaganda-red ring, with a small five-point star separating the two arcs on each side. A small olive tab at the very bottom reads **'PBI 34520'**.

**Center emblem:** a **retro-futurist military transport aircraft lifting off — climbing steeply with its landing gear retracting (wheels up)** as the hero device. It is a WW2 C-47 Skytrain / Dakota silhouette reimagined with cyberpunk touches (twin engines rendered as faintly cyan-glowing thruster-rings, olive-drab body with invasion stripes), nose angled up and away over a short stretch of stencilled runway. A small stamped **payload crate marked 'RC'** rides in its belly, and a single **green launch flare / GO light** glows at the base of the runway (the one permitted green accent). Speed-lines and a thin contrail sweep behind it. Essence: the build is loaded, the ramp is clear, wheels are up — it ships.

**Unit DNA (shared across the set):** integrate a small parachute-over-drop-zone-marker mark — the VDS Pathfinder Detachment emblem — as a subtle motif near the bottom-center of the disc, with a faint cyan glow on the parachute shroud lines. Keep it small; the lifting-transport central emblem dominates.

**Critical:** one or two symbols maximum (the climbing transport + the runway/GO light), no human figures, no scene clutter. The rising plane with wheels retracting must read instantly as *launch / wheels up / it ships*, not as a generic airliner or a crash. Keep the green confined to the single GO flare and the cyan confined to the thruster-ring highlight — do not let either spread into the field; the warm parchment / burnt-orange / olive / propaganda-red backbone must dominate. The aged-paper / embroidered-patch finish must survive — if it reads as a glossy modern app icon or Cyberpunk fan art, pull the neon back and push the propaganda-patch framing forward.
