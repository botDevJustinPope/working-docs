**Operation insignia — circular mission patch (Teams thread image).**

**Format:** A single circular military unit insignia / mission patch, centered and filling the frame on a flat dark-charcoal or transparent background, sized to crop cleanly into a round Teams avatar. Square 1:1 canvas. Bold and iconographic — it must stay legible at small avatar size.

**Style:** WW2-era squadron embroidered-patch / stamped-leather emblem fused with subtle retro-futurist cyberpunk. Aged, lightly distressed, with a stitched double-border rim. Palette: parchment cream (#E8D9B0–#F2E5C1), burnt orange (#C5602D–#E0813C), olive drab (#5A6342–#7B7B4A), propaganda red (#A8341F–#C53A22), deep brown (#3E2A1C). Sparing electric-cyan glow (#3FB3D8) as an Era III accent only — a thin highlight, never dominant. No photorealism, no modern flat-design pastels, no pure-white background.

**Rim:** an outer ring carrying curved stencil ALL-CAPS text — **'OPERATION ROOT CAUSE'** across the top arc and **'TRACE THE FAULT · FIND THE ROOT'** across the bottom arc — cream lettering on a propaganda-red ring. A small olive tab at the very bottom reads **'PBI 34203'**.

**Center emblem:** a single fault-trace line rendered as a glowing cyan signal-wire that starts at a small red alarm spark at the top (the reported symptom) and runs downward, branching once like a root, ending at a tree-root / taproot motif anchored in the soil at the base of the disc — symbol of tracing a fault from symptom down to its true root. Optionally a small magnifier or a stamped tag near the root reading **"SOURCE"**. Essence: don't blame the surface — follow the fault down to the root. One clean trace from spark to root; no scene clutter.

**Unit DNA (shared across the set):** integrate a small parachute-over-drop-zone-marker mark — the VDS Pathfinder Detachment emblem — as a subtle motif near the bottom-center of the disc, with a faint cyan glow on the parachute shroud lines. Keep it small; the central root-trace emblem dominates.

**Critical:** one or two symbols maximum (the spark-to-root trace line + optional "SOURCE" tag), no human figures, no scene clutter. The cyan glow stays confined to the trace-wire — do not let it spread into the field; the warm parchment / orange / olive / red backbone must still dominate. The aged-paper / embroidered-patch finish must survive — if it reads as a glossy modern app icon or Cyberpunk fan art, pull the neon back and push the propaganda-patch framing forward.
