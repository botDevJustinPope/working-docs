**Operation insignia — circular mission patch (Teams thread image).**

**Format:** A single circular military unit insignia / mission patch, centered and filling the frame on a flat dark-charcoal or transparent background, sized to crop cleanly into a round Teams avatar. Square 1:1 canvas. Bold and iconographic — it must stay legible at small avatar size.

**Style:** WW2-era squadron embroidered-patch / stamped-leather emblem fused with subtle retro-futurist cyberpunk. Aged, lightly distressed, with a stitched double-border rim. Palette: parchment cream (#E8D9B0–#F2E5C1), burnt orange (#C5602D–#E0813C), olive drab (#5A6342–#7B7B4A), propaganda red (#A8341F–#C53A22), deep brown (#3E2A1C). Sparing electric-cyan glow (#3FB3D8) as an Era III accent only — a thin highlight, never dominant. No photorealism, no modern flat-design pastels, no pure-white background.

**Rim:** an outer ring carrying curved stencil ALL-CAPS text — **'OPERATION CLEAN SWEEP'** across the top arc and **'DONE AND DUSTED'** across the bottom arc — cream lettering on a propaganda-red ring, with a small five-point star separating the two arcs on each side. A small olive tab at the very bottom reads **'BUG 34256'**.

**Center emblem:** a **push-broom crossed with a wrench** (the broom for the clean sweep, the wrench for Ironforge's fix) sweeping a small **'DMH' product-card** clean — a little red **'CANNOT SELECT'** error-tag is being knocked off the card in a curl of dust and speed-streaks, while the card's product tile flips to a green **'SELECTED ✓'** check. Tucked into the lower field, a tiny **stopwatch frozen near zero** signals the same-day speed. Essence: a high-priority hotfix swept clean in a single fast stroke — in and out before the dust settled.

**Unit DNA (shared across the set):** integrate a small parachute-over-drop-zone-marker mark — the VDS Pathfinder Detachment emblem — as a subtle motif near the bottom-center of the disc, with a faint cyan glow on the parachute shroud lines. Keep it small; the broom-and-wrench central emblem dominates.

**Critical:** one or two symbols maximum (broom-and-wrench + the swept card), no human figures, no scene clutter. The dust-and-speed motion should read instantly as "fast." The cyan glow stays confined to the green/cyan SELECTED check — do not let it spread into the field; the warm parchment / burnt-orange / olive / propaganda-red backbone must dominate. The aged-paper / embroidered-patch finish must survive — if it reads as a glossy modern app icon or Cyberpunk fan art, pull the neon back and push the propaganda-patch framing forward.
