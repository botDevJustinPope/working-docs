**Operation insignia — circular mission patch (Teams thread image).**

**Format:** A single circular military unit insignia / mission patch, centered and filling the frame on a flat dark-charcoal or transparent background, sized to crop cleanly into a round Teams avatar. Square 1:1 canvas. Bold and iconographic — it must stay legible at small avatar size.

**Style:** WW2-era squadron embroidered-patch / stamped-leather emblem fused with subtle retro-futurist cyberpunk. Aged, lightly distressed, with a stitched double-border rim. Palette: parchment cream (#E8D9B0–#F2E5C1), burnt orange (#C5602D–#E0813C), olive drab (#5A6342–#7B7B4A), propaganda red (#A8341F–#C53A22), deep brown (#3E2A1C). Sparing electric-cyan glow (#3FB3D8) as an Era III accent only — a thin highlight, never dominant. No photorealism, no modern flat-design pastels, no pure-white background.

**Rim:** an outer ring carrying curved stencil ALL-CAPS text — **'OPERATION TOURNIQUET'** across the top arc and **'STOP THE BLEEDING'** across the bottom arc — cream lettering on a propaganda-red ring, with a small five-point star separating the two arcs on each side. A small olive tab at the very bottom reads **'6/26 HOTFIX'**.

**Center emblem:** a **field tourniquet cinched tight** as the hero device — a rubber-and-brass strap with a wooden/brass **windlass rod twisted to clamp** — wrapped around a **bleeding code-line / server-rack "limb,"** stemming a propaganda-red leak to a stop (a couple of stylized red drops caught mid-fall, the flow pinched off above them). Behind the tourniquet sits a subtle WW2 **field-medic cross** (cream roundel, red cross) marking the aid station. Essence: a production wound clamped shut fast under pressure — cinch first, ship the patch.

**Unit DNA (shared across the set):** integrate a small parachute-over-drop-zone-marker mark — the VDS Pathfinder Detachment emblem — as a subtle motif near the bottom-center of the disc, with a faint cyan glow on the parachute shroud lines. Keep it small; the tourniquet central emblem dominates.

**Critical:** one or two symbols maximum (the cinched tourniquet + the medic cross), no human figures, no scene clutter. The propaganda-red "bleed" should read instantly as "stopped/clamped," not gory — keep it stylized and graphic. The cyan glow stays a thin accent only; the warm parchment / burnt-orange / olive / propaganda-red backbone must dominate. The aged-paper / embroidered-patch finish must survive — if it reads as a glossy modern app icon or Cyberpunk fan art, pull the neon back and push the propaganda-patch framing forward.
