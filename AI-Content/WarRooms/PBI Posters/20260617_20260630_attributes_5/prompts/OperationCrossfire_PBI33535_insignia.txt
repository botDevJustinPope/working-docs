**Operation insignia — circular mission patch (Teams thread image).**

**Format:** A single circular military unit insignia / mission patch, centered and filling the frame on a flat dark-charcoal or transparent background, sized to crop cleanly into a round Teams avatar. Square 1:1 canvas. Bold and iconographic — it must stay legible at small avatar size.

**Style:** WW2-era squadron embroidered-patch / stamped-leather emblem fused with subtle retro-futurist cyberpunk. Aged, lightly distressed, with a stitched double-border rim. Palette: parchment cream (#E8D9B0–#F2E5C1), burnt orange (#C5602D–#E0813C), olive drab (#5A6342–#7B7B4A), propaganda red (#A8341F–#C53A22), deep brown (#3E2A1C). Sparing electric-cyan glow (#3FB3D8) as an Era III accent only — a thin highlight, never dominant. No photorealism, no modern flat-design pastels, no pure-white background.

**Rim:** an outer ring carrying curved stencil ALL-CAPS text — **'OPERATION CROSSFIRE'** across the top arc and **'STOP POINTING · READ THE LOGS'** across the bottom arc — cream lettering on a propaganda-red ring. A small olive tab at the very bottom reads **'PBI 33535'**.

**Center emblem:** an open, **EMPTY** steel image-crate / film canister stenciled **'GPC'** at the center, its lid ajar, with three stylized accusing hands (or arrows) curving around it in a ring — each pointing at the next, the Mexican-standoff blame circle rendered as a single circular motif with no resolution. A thin glowing cyan **log-tape ribbon** unspools beneath the crate — the one honest witness, ignored. Essence: a shipment of images that never arrived, and a circle of blame with zero confessions.

**Unit DNA (shared across the set):** integrate a small parachute-over-drop-zone-marker mark — the VDS Pathfinder Detachment emblem — as a subtle motif near the bottom-center of the disc, with a faint cyan glow on the parachute shroud lines. Keep it small; the central emblem dominates.

**Critical:** one or two symbols maximum, no human figures, no scene clutter. The cyan glow stays confined to the log-tape ribbon — do not let it spread into the field; the warm parchment / orange / olive / red backbone must still dominate. The aged-paper / embroidered-patch finish must survive — if it reads as a glossy modern app icon or Cyberpunk fan art, pull the neon back and push the propaganda-patch framing forward.
