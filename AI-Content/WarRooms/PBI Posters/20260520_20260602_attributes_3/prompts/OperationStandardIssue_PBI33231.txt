Aged-paper texture and distressed print grain over a parchment-cream backbone, with burnt orange, olive drab, propaganda red, and deep brown dominating; electric cyan and holographic blue used sparingly as accents only. Volumetric fog, rain, sparks, and faint warm-toned code rain in the background. Painterly illustration with cinematic chiaroscuro lighting — stylized realism, not photoreal. Portrait 2:3 aspect ratio.
