YukonConnection is the connection string that the VDS application uses to connect striaght to the Echelon server and the WBS database for veodesignstudio.com, for other stacks take a look at the libary for the appsetting by navigating to Pipelines>Library. With this connection string we call the following procedures listed out in the following sections. The purpose was to:
- Identify what we are calling directly
- trace its usage (actaul sql sever call => endpoint)

_This does not get into where in the app does the enpdoints get used which could  be in a later wiki or story to figure out._
